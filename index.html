Who We Are

Post 517 is part of The American Legion, chartered in 1919 to serve veterans, families, and the community. We offer a welcoming place for veterans to connect, volunteer, and strengthen the Lake Tawakoni area.

As a nonprofit veterans organization and community hub, we host food nights, karaoke, fundraisers, and gatherings for veterans, families, and friends in a friendly, active, and welcoming environment.

Our Mission

Committed to serving those who served, strengthening America, and upholding the principles of justice, freedom, and democracy through veterans affairs, national security, Americanism, and children & youth programs.

Four Pillars of Service

The American Legion's mission rests on four foundational pillars that guide our work at Post 517

Veterans Affairs & Rehabilitation

Support services, benefits assistance, peer support, and crisis prevention resources for veterans in need.

National Security

Supporting active-duty military, National Guard, Reserve members, and advocating for a strong national defense.

Americanism

Fostering patriotism, respect for the flag, civic responsibility, and celebrating American values through community engagement.

Children & Youth

Scholarships, Boys State, American Legion Baseball, mentorship, and support for the next generation.
